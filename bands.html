Crooked Weather

Crooked Weather fresh from their European tour, play earthy, unruly and original folk songs. The sound is led by carefully crafted lyrics delivered by the lilting harmonies of Holly and Will. These songs echo the ethos of folk songsmiths before them with imagery of escape, adventure, companionship and inner searching but told from a different heart.
